Shershaah (2021)

About Soundtrack Gallery

About Shershaah

Synopsis: A biographical war drama on Param Vir Chakra awardee Capt. Vikram Batra, call-sign "Shershaah", covering his 13 JAK Rifles service in the 1999 Kargil War, and his relationship with Dimple Cheema.

Dialog / Taglines

"Yeh Dil Maange More!"
"Either I will come back after hoisting the Tricolor, or I will come back wrapped in it."

Cast (List)

  • Director: Vishnuvardhan
  • Vikram/Vishal Batra: Sidharth Malhotra
  • Dimple Cheema: Kiara Advani
  • Lt. Col. Y.K. Joshi: Shataf Figar
  • Capt. Sanjeev Jamwal: Shiv Pandit
  • Sunny: Sahil Vaid
